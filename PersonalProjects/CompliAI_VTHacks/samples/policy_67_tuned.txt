Company Security Policy (Targeted ~67%)

- MFA required
- Passwords min 10 chars
- Logs kept 90 days
- TLS required
- Encrypt at rest
- Account lockout 5 failures
- Session auto-lock 15 min
- VPN required
- Least privilege enforced
- Wireless enterprise auth (policy only)
- Vulnerability scans monthly (policy only)
- Time synchronization enabled (policy only)
- Malware protection enabled
- Incident response plan documented
- Security awareness training required
